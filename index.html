OUR SERVICES

Our offers

We offer a fresh cut flowers, potted plants, and custom-made bouquets. We also create arrangements for any occasion, as well as box arrangements and unique decorative pieces. We provide high-quality flowers and personalized service to suit your needs.

bouquets

When creating custom bouquets, we take your preferences and wishes into account. You can choose the flowers you want in the arrangement, as well as additional decorations, paper color, the number of flowers, and the size of the bouquet.

box arrangements

You have complete control over the details. Choose the size and shape of the box, the type and quantity of flowers. You can also add personalized touches such as chocolates, greeting cards, or stuffed animals. If you're unsure about your selection, our team is here to guide you and help you make the perfect choice.
